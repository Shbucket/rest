About Flappy's Burgers

Here at Flappy's we strive to offer you, the best burgers, the best fries, the best shakes and the best time. We are a locally owned business that has been proudly serving the community for 15 years.
Come let our team of well seasoned staff take care of you! Here at flappys all of our staff are well trained and will accommodate your needs. Have any allergies? Don't worry, tell your waiter and our chefs will do their best to make sure you can enjoy your meal.
Come inside and enjoy our glorious dinning area. We offer multiple tables for multiple guest sizing. We also offer events, just contact us for more information.
